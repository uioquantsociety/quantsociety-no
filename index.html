About us

UIO Quant Society brings together students who enjoy math, computer science, economics, and statistics. We build skills used in finance: data analysis, modelling, optimisation, and risk management. Just as important, we keep the vibe friendly and low-pressure so it’s easy to learn and share.

Our motto is “a bridge between campus and work life.” You’ll get practice building and testing strategies, working in teams, and presenting results.

What to expect

Learning

Workshops, talks, and small projects across quant, data analysis, and programming.

Community

Sparring and discussion across disciplines. Share experiences, build competence, learn from each other.

Career connections

Company meetups, case evenings, and concrete tips for CVs and interviews — a direct link to relevant employers.

Activities

Algorithmic trading

We develop, backtest, and discuss trading strategies in Python. Focus on clean data, signals, risk, and robustness.

Interview prep

Brain teasers, probability, statistics, financial math, and coding — the type of problems you’ll meet in quant interviews.

Social evenings

Open discussion, share progress and ideas, socialize with other quants.
